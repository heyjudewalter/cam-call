Community Rules

By using CamCall, you agree to follow these rules.

  1. Be respectful to everyone. Treat others how you want to be treated.
  2. Keep all content family-friendly at all times.
  3. No exploits, hacks, or attempts to disrupt the service.
  4. Do not share personal information about yourself or others.
  5. Do not abuse owner/call moderation permissions.
  6. No illegal content of any kind.
  7. No inappropriate, explicit, or NSFW content.
  8. No spamming, flooding, or disruptive behavior in chats.
  9. Do not impersonate other users or misrepresent your identity.
  10. Use screen sharing responsibly — do not share private or sensitive content.
  11. Follow the instructions of call owners and moderators.
  12. Have fun and be a good community member.
Enforcement: Violating these rules may result in a warning, kick, ban, or IP ban at the discretion of the call owner. Repeated or severe violations may result in permanent removal from the platform.
